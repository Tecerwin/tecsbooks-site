  FAQ  

The usual questions.

 
 
  Do you do taxes too?  
Bookkeeping prepares clean records for your tax pro. We can coordinate with your CPA.
 
 
  What do you need from me each month?  
Typically: bank/credit access, any invoices/receipts not in the system, and answers to a few quick questions.
 
 
  Can you work with my current QuickBooks file?  
Yep. We can clean it up and keep it maintained going forward.
 
 
  Do you work with clients outside San Diego?  
Yes—remote is fine. San Diego is just home base.
